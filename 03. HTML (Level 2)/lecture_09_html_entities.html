HTML Entities

  • An HTML entity is a piece of text ("String") that begins with an ampersand (&) and ends with a semicolon (;).

  • Used to display reserved characters (which whould otherwise be interpreted as HTML code), and invisible characters (like non-breaking spaces).

  • Can also use in place of characters that are difficult to type with a standard keyboard.

  • Browser interprets them & render correct character.
