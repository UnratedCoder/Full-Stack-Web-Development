Semantic Markup

It is the markup that relates to the meaning of content

Why we use semantic markup tags?

  1. Meaningful / Layout ⇒ Structured
  2. SEO Friendly ⇒ (SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization)
  3. Websites are readable + screen Reader (for blind people) ⇒ UX (User Experience) improved

There are 2 types of Tags in HTML

  • Semantic Tag ⇒ Meaning of tag

    for eg: heading tag, paragraph tag, image tag, video tag, etc...

  • Non-Semantic Tag ⇒ Non Meaning of tag

    for eg: div, span ect..
