}

Padding Shorthand

  1. for all 4 sides

    padding: 50px;

  2. top & bottom | left &right

    padding: 1px 2px;

  3. top | left & right | bottom

    padding: 1px 2px 3px;

  4. top | right | bottom | left

    padding: 1px 2px 3px 4px;
